Definiciones de currency
Sustantivo
a system of money in general use in a particular country.
the dollar was a strong currency
sinónimos: money, legal tender, cash, banknotes, bills, notes, coins, coinage, specie
the fact or quality of being generally accepted or in use.
the term gained currency during the second half of the 20th century
sinónimos: prevalence, circulation, exposure, acceptance, popularity
